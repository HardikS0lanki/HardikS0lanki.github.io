Profile Summary

I am an enthusiastic and results-driven Senior Security Engineer with over 6 years of progressive experience in Application and Product Security, combining deep expertise in both offensive and defensive security practices. I specialize in Threat Modeling, Secure SDLC/SSDLC, Security Architecture, and Vulnerability Management—embedding security seamlessly into the product lifecycle from design to deployment with a strong shift-left approach. My hands-on experience spans Secure Design Reviews, Secure Coding Practices, Penetration Testing, and Compliance & Auditing, with a strong command of leading industry standards such as OWASP Top 10, OWASP ASVS, NIST, and CWE/SANS Top 25.

Passionate about modern DevSecOps practices, I focus on automating security testing, integrating robust security controls into CI/CD pipelines, and fostering a culture of secure coding and engineering excellence. Skilled in risk identification and mitigation across infrastructure and application layers, I am recognized for resolving security issues within defined SLAs, clear cross-functional communication, and a collaborative approach that strengthens organizational security posture. I am eager to contribute to innovative, security-first initiatives in forward-thinking environments.
